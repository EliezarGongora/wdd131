The first photograph: The first photograph was taken in 1826 by Joseph Nicéphore Niépce, a French scientist, using a technique called heliography. The photograph was titled View from the Window at Le Gras.

Elements of a good photograph: The three most important elements of a good photograph are light, subject, and composition.

The word "photography": The word "photography" comes from Greek and means "to draw with light"
